From enquiry to live.

A clear, predictable five-stage process. You know what's happening, when, and why — no agency fog. Most projects ship in eight to ten weeks.

01

Discovery

A 60-minute discovery call, brand & competitor audit, and a written brief you sign off before we touch design. No briefs done by email tennis.

1 week
02

Design

Wireframes first, then a full design system. Two structured rounds of feedback. You sign off on every page before code is written.

2–3 weeks
03

Build

Production-grade HTML/CSS/JS. Lighthouse benchmarked daily. Content loaded together. Staging environment open from week one.

3–4 weeks
04

Launch

Pre-launch QA, staging review, training session for your team, DNS cutover. We're on the keyboard for the first 48 hours.

1 week
05

Care

Hosting, security, backups, content edits, ongoing optimisation. Pro and Premium include this — Basic upgrades from $90/mo.
